How to Install Covenant on Kodi Firestick?

Follow these simple steps to install Covenant on Kodi Firestick:

  1. Ensure that your Firestick device is connected to the internet.
  2. Open Kodi and navigate to the ‘Settings’ menu.
  3. Click on ‘System’ and then click on ‘Add-ons’.
  4. Enable the ‘Unknown sources’ option and click ‘Yes’ to confirm.
  5. Return to the ‘Settings’ menu and click on ‘File Manager’.
  6. Click on ‘Add source’ and then select ‘None’.
  7. Type in the URL ‘http://archive.org/download/repository.xvbmc/’ and click ‘Ok’.
  8. Enter the name ‘XvBMC’ and click ‘Ok’.
  9. Go back to the Kodi home screen and select ‘Add-ons’.
  10. Click on the ‘Package Installer’ icon and select ‘Install from zip file’.
  11. Select ‘XvBMC’ and click on the file ‘repository.xvbmc-4.2.zip’.
  12. Wait for the addon to install and then click on ‘Install from repository’.
  13. Select the ‘XvBMC (Add-ons)’ repository and click on ‘Video Add-Ons’.
  14. Scroll down and select ‘Covenant’ and click on ‘Install’.
  15. Wait for the addon to install, and once completed, a message will appear on the screen.
  16. You can now access Covenant from the ‘Add-ons’ menu on the Kodi home screen.
