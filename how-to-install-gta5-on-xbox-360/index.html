The process of installing GTA5 on an Xbox 360 can be broken down into a few simple steps. Firstly, make sure that you have a stable internet connection to get the updates. Installing GTA5 on an Xbox 360 can also be done with a USB drive. Firstly, connect the USB drive to your computer and format it with the FAT32 file system. Lastly, insert the USB drive into the Xbox 360 console and follow the on-screen prompts to complete the installation process.
