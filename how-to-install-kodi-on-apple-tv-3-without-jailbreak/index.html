How to install Kodi on Apple TV 3 without jailbreak

Installing Kodi on your Apple TV 3 without jailbreaking might seem difficult, but with a few simple steps, you’ll have it up and running in no time. First, you need a few things:

  • A computer with Xcode installed
  • The latest version of Kodi (as well as a compatible version for the Apple TV 3)
  • A USB cable
  • The knowledge of sideloading apps

Once you have these things, follow these steps:

  1. Connect your Apple TV 3 to your computer using a USB cable.
  2. Open Xcode on your computer and select “Create a new Xcode project.”
  3. Select “Application” and choose a name and identifier for your app. It can be any name you choose since this is just for identifying your app.
  4. In the next screen, select “Apple TV” and “Single View Application,” and then click “Next.”
  5. Choose a location to save your project, and click “Create.”
  6. Add your Apple TV to the list of simulators by clicking on the “Window” drop-down menu, selecting “Devices and Simulators,” and then clicking the plus button in the bottom left-hand corner of the screen.
  7. Drag the Kodi IPA file into the “Embedded Binaries section” of your project.
  8. Under “General” in the left-hand menu of Xcode, change the “Bundle Identifier” to match the one you created in step 3.
  9. Select your Apple TV in the list of simulators to the left of the “Play” button.
  10. Click the “Play” button in the top left-hand corner of the Xcode window.
  11. Wait for Xcode to install Kodi on your Apple TV.
  12. Once Xcode is finished, you can use Kodi on your Apple TV.

Summary of how to install Kodi on Apple TV 3 without jailbreak

How to install Kodi add-ons

To install Kodi add-ons:

  1. Open Kodi and go to “Add-ons”.
  2. Select the add-on browser icon.
  3. Select “Install from repository.”
  4. Select the add-on you want to install.
  5. Click “Install” on the add-on’s information screen.
  6. Wait for the add-on to install and then access it from the “Add-ons” menu.
