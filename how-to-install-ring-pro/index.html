If you’re looking to increase the security of your home and keep an eye on who’s at your front door, you may be interested in installing a Ring Pro doorbell. Not only does this smart doorbell offer a sleek design, it also comes with a number of features to keep your home safe. But how do you install it? Read on to learn more about how to install Ring Pro and get started with improved home security.

Installing a doorbell can be a daunting task, especially if you don’t have experience with wiring or working with electronics. Additionally, if you have Dutch lap siding or other non-standard features on your home, you may be worried about how to mount the doorbell correctly. Don’t worry, though - with the right tools and a little bit of know-how, you’ll be able to install Ring Pro quickly and easily.

The first step in how to install Ring Pro is to ensure that your existing doorbell wiring is compatible with the smart doorbell. If your current doorbell isn’t wired, you’ll need to hire an electrician to install a compatible transformer. Once you have the right wiring in place, you’ll need to remove your existing doorbell and attach the mounting bracket for Ring Pro to your home’s siding or trim.

Next, connect the wires from your doorbell to the corresponding terminals on the back of the Ring Pro. Then, attach the faceplate to the mounting bracket and tighten the security screws. Finally, download the Ring app and follow the instructions to connect your new doorbell to your home’s Wi-Fi network. Once everything is set up, you’ll be able to view live video and use all of the features of your Ring Pro.

My Experience with Installing Ring Pro

When I first purchased Ring Pro, I was intimidated by the installation process. However, I found that the instructions provided by Ring were clear and easy to follow. The most challenging part of the process for me was mounting the doorbell to my Dutch lap siding, since I had to use additional adapters to ensure that the camera would be level and flush with the siding. However, with a little bit of extra effort, I was able to mount my Ring Pro securely and it has been working perfectly ever since.

Tips for Installing Ring Pro on Non-Standard Features

If you have Dutch lap siding or other non-standard features on your home, don’t worry - you can still install Ring Pro. Ring offers a number of different adapters and mounting solutions to help ensure that your doorbell is level and secure. If you’re having trouble finding the right mounting solution for your home, consider contacting Ring’s customer support team for assistance.

Mounting Ring Pro to Stucco or Brick

If you have stucco or brick on your home, you may be wondering how to install Ring Pro without damaging your siding. One option is to use a wedge kit, which allows you to install the doorbell at an angle so that it’s flush with your siding. Alternatively, you can use a mounting plate to attach the Ring Pro directly to your siding without drilling any holes.

Connecting Ring Pro to Amazon Alexa

If you have an Amazon Alexa device, you can connect it to your Ring Pro to receive announcements when someone rings your doorbell. To set this up, open the Alexa app and navigate to the devices tab. From there, select your Ring Pro and enable doorbell announcements. You can also view live video from your Ring Pro on your Alexa device by asking Alexa to show you the front door.

Questions and Answers about how to install Ring Pro

Q: Do I need to have an existing doorbell in order to install Ring Pro?
A: Yes, you’ll need to have existing doorbell wiring in place in order to install Ring Pro.

Q: Can I install Ring Pro on my own, or do I need to hire a professional?
A: While you don’t necessarily need to hire a professional, it’s recommended that you have at least some experience with electrical wiring before attempting to install Ring Pro on your own.

Q: Does Ring Pro come with all of the necessary mounting hardware?
A: Yes, Ring Pro comes with a mounting bracket and screws to attach it to your home’s siding or trim.

Q: Do I need to have a Ring Protect subscription in order to use all of the features of Ring Pro?
A: No, you don’t need a Ring Protect subscription to use all of the basic features of Ring Pro. However, you may want to consider a subscription in order to store video recordings or receive additional alerts.

Conclusion of how to install Ring Pro

Overall, installing Ring Pro is a straightforward process that can be completed by most homeowners with a little bit of effort. By following the steps outlined above and taking advantage of the mounting solutions available from Ring, you can enjoy the added security and convenience of this smart doorbell in no time.
