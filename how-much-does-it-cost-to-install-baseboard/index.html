Installing baseboard can add a touch of elegance to any room. However, it can be quite challenging to determine how much it costs to install baseboard. Fear not! In this post, we will be exploring the costs associated with installing baseboard. So, if you’re wondering how much it costs to install baseboard, keep reading.

Pain Points of Installing Baseboard

One of the most significant pain points of installing baseboard is the cost. Baseboards can get expensive, especially if you are looking for high-quality materials. Other factors that can influence the cost include the size of the room and the type of baseboard selected.

How Much Does It Cost to Install Baseboard?

The cost of installing baseboard can vary depending on different factors such as the type of baseboard, size of the room, and labor cost. On average, the cost typically ranges from $200 to $600 per room. However, if you factor in higher-quality materials, the cost can be as high as $1,200 per room. It’s always wise to get a quote from a professional to get a more accurate estimate.

Summary

Installing baseboard can add value and elegance to your home. However, the cost can vary depending on different factors such as the type of baseboard, size of the room, and labor cost. Always get a quote from a professional before starting the project.

How to Save Money on Installing Baseboard

If you’re looking to save money on installing baseboard, you could consider installing the baseboard yourself. However, if you’re not comfortable with DIY, you could look for baseboards that are affordable, yet good quality. Another option is to consider hiring a contractor who charges an hourly rate, rather than a fixed price. This method can help you save money, especially if you have a small room.

The Type of Baseboard Matters

The type of baseboard you select can significantly impact the cost of installation. Composite, MDF, and PVC baseboards are less expensive, while solid wood baseboards are more expensive. Homeowners who are looking to cut costs should consider a composite, MDF, or PVC baseboard option.

Labor Costs

Labor costs can also impact the cost of installing baseboard. Typically, labor cost can range from $60 to $120 per hour. If you want to save on labor, consider installing the baseboard yourself if you have the skills and time.

FAQs About Installing Baseboards

Q: Can I install baseboard without removing the old one?

A: While it’s not technically impossible, it’s best to remove the old baseboard before installing a new one. This ensures a smooth and seamless installation and avoids unsightly gaps.

Q: How long does it take to install baseboard?

A: The length of time it takes to install baseboard can vary depending on the size of the room, the type of baseboard, and the skill level of the installer involved. However, most installations can be completed in four to six hours, which includes prep work, installation, and cleanup.

Q: What tools do I need to install baseboard?

A: The tools needed for installing baseboard include a measuring tape, miter saw, sawhorses, finish nails, air compressor, and a nail gun. You may also need a level, drill, and wood filler.

Q: Can I paint my baseboards after installation?

A: Yes! In fact, it’s a great way to give your room a fresh look. Ensure to prime the baseboards before applying the paint to give the paint a good surface to adhere to.

Conclusion of How Much Does It Cost to Install Baseboard

Installing baseboard may seem costly, but it’s a worthwhile investment that can add elegance and value to your home. Remember, factors such as the size of the room, the type of baseboard you select, and labor cost can impact the total cost of installation. Consider getting quotes from different contractors and look for ways to save money, such as doing it yourself, if you are comfortable with DIY.
