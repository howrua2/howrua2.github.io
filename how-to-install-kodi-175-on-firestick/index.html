If you’re a fan of streaming content on your TV, you’ve likely heard of Kodi. This popular media player and streaming app can be installed on a variety of devices, including the Amazon Firestick. Though Kodi is user-friendly, installing it on the Firestick can be a little tricky. In this article, we’ll show you how to install Kodi 17.5 on Firestick in just a few easy steps.

Why installing Kodi 17.5 on Firestick might be difficult

Kodi is not available on the Amazon App Store, which can make installing it more difficult than installing other apps. Additionally, if you don’t have experience sideloading apps onto your Firestick, the process can seem daunting.

How to install Kodi 17.5 on Firestick

First, you need to enable apps from unknown sources on your Firestick. To do so, go to Settings > Device or My Fire TV > Developer Options > Apps from Unknown Sources. Toggle the switch to enable this option.

Next, download the ES File Explorer app from the App Store. Open ES File Explorer and select the Downloader option from the Tools menu. In the URL bar, enter the following link: https://kodi.tv/download/803 and download the Kodi 17.5 APK file.

Once the APK file has downloaded, open it and select Install. The app will begin installing on your Firestick. Once installation is complete, you can launch Kodi from your Apps list.

Main Points

In summary, to install Kodi 17.5 on Firestick, you need to enable apps from unknown sources, download the ES File Explorer app, and download the Kodi 17.5 APK file. Once you’ve installed the APK file, you can launch Kodi from your Apps list.

Troubleshooting Common Issues when Installing Kodi 17.5 on Firestick

If you’re having issues installing Kodi 17.5 on your Firestick, there are a few things you can try. First, double-check that you’ve enabled apps from unknown sources and downloaded the ES File Explorer app. Additionally, try clearing your Firestick’s cache or restarting your Firestick. Lastly, be sure you’re downloading the correct version of the Kodi APK file for your Firestick.
