Are you considering upgrading your home by installing baseboards but are unsure of the cost? This article will cover all you need to know about how much to install baseboard. From the cost of materials to the installation process, we’ve got you covered.

Pain Points

Baseboards are a crucial home aesthetic that goes unnoticed. They aid in hiding uneven gaps between the flooring and the wall and protect the walls from scuffs and scratches caused by foot and furniture traffic. Still, the cost of installation can be a significant factor preventing homeowners from upgrading their homes.

How Much to Install Baseboard?

The cost to install baseboard ranges from $1 to $6 per linear foot, depending on the materials used, labor costs, and the size of the room. For instance, installing baseboards for a 150 linear foot room with zero fancy cuts and simple materials may cost between $150 and $900. On the other hand, rooms featuring floors with uneven and curved walls require more materials and labor, increasing the cost of installation.

Main Points

In summary, how much to install baseboard depends on several factors, including the materials used, labor costs, and size of the room. Installing baseboards can complete the look of your home and offer valuable protection to your walls. By being aware of the costs involved, you can make an informed decision about your upgrade.

Personal Experience

After renovating my home, I wanted to enhance the look of my new floors by installing baseboards. The cost of installation was a concern, but after researching the materials and labor costs, I was surprised to find the entire project was more cost-effective than I anticipated. I was thrilled with the final results, and I can now welcome guests into my home with pride.

Material Costs

When it comes to materials, there are several options available. The most popular materials used include wood, PVC, and MDF. PVC is the least expensive option, and wood is the most expensive. MDF falls in the middle, offering a durable and affordable material option.

Labor Costs

The cost for labor can range from $1 to $3 per linear foot, depending on the expertise of the installer and the complexity of the installation.

Installation Process

The installation process typically involves the following: choosing the right baseboard material, measuring the room’s perimeter, cutting the materials to size, and then attaching them to the wall using nails or glue. While it is possible to install baseboards as a do-it-yourself project, it’s recommended that you hire a professional installer for precise and efficient results.

FAQs

Q: Is it necessary to remove old baseboards before installation?
A: It is not required to remove the old baseboards before installation, but it is recommended for a cleaner look.

Q: How long does it take to install baseboards in a room?
A: The length of time required for installation depends on the size of the room and the type of baseboard being installed.

Q: Can I paint baseboards after installation?
A: Yes, baseboards can be painted after installation to match the color scheme of the room.

Q: How do I know what size baseboards to purchase?
A: Measure the room’s perimeter and select baseboards that are within your budget and preferred style.

Conclusion of How Much to Install Baseboard

By understanding the materials and labor costs involved, you can upgrade your home with beautiful baseboards that offer valuable protection to your walls without breaking the bank. Baseboards enhance the look and feel of your home, and with the help of a professional installer, you can enjoy the finished results worry-free.
