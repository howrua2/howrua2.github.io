How to Play Xbox One Games Without Installing Using the “Instant On” Feature

As mentioned earlier, the “Instant On” feature is one way to play Xbox One games without installing. This feature allows you to resume your game from where you left off without having to install it again. Here’s how to use it:

To turn on the “Instant On” feature, go to “Settings” > “Power & startup” > “Power mode & startup”. From here, select “Instant-on” as your power mode. Now, when you turn off your Xbox One, it will go into a low-power state instead of fully shutting down. When you turn it back on, you can resume your game from where you left off without having to install it again.

How to Play Xbox One Games Without Installing Using Cloud Gaming Services

Another way to play Xbox One games without installing is to use cloud gaming services such as Xbox Game Pass Ultimate and xCloud. With these services, you can stream games directly to your device without needing to install them. Here’s how to use them:

First, sign up for Xbox Game Pass Ultimate or xCloud. Once you’ve signed up, you can access a library of games that you can stream directly to your Xbox One or other compatible device. You’ll need a stable internet connection to play games through these services, but it’s a great way to play games without taking up space on your device.
