How to Install Kodi on New Firestick

To install Kodi on your new Firestick, follow these steps:

  1. Go to your Firestick home screen and click on the “Settings” tab.
  2. Select the “My Fire TV” option and click on “Developer Options”.
  3. Turn on the “Apps from Unknown Sources” option.
  4. Go back to your home screen and click on the “Search” tab.
  5. Type in “Downloader” and select the app from the list that appears.
  6. Download and install the “Downloader” app.
  7. Open the “Downloader” app and type in the following URL: http://kodi.tv/download
  8. Select the appropriate version of Kodi for your Firestick, then click “Download”.
  9. Wait for Kodi to finish downloading and installing.
  10. Go back to your Firestick home screen and launch Kodi.
