Installing a new thermostat can be a daunting task, and having to do it yourself can be even more intimidating. But fear not, as we have compiled a guide to help you install the ecobee3 smart thermostat hassle-free.

First things first, make sure you turn off the power supply to your heating and cooling equipment before you begin the installation process. Next, remove the old thermostat and check for any corrosion or damage. If there is, be sure to fix it before you proceed with the installation of your new ecobee3.

Once you’ve confirmed that your old thermostat is ready for replacement, it’s time to install the new device. Start by mounting the backplate on the wall and connecting the wires to the appropriate terminals. If you’re unsure about which wire goes where, refer to the manual or contact a professional. After the wiring is done, attach the ecobee3 thermostat to the backplate and turn on the power supply. Your ecobee3 is now ready to use!

This simple installation process will help you save money and make your home more comfortable in no time. Remember, it’s important to follow the instructions carefully and ensure that you turn off the power supply before you begin the installation process. Once installed, the ecobee3 will provide you with a user-friendly experience and help you save money on your energy bill while keeping your home comfortable at all times.

Tips for installing ecobee3

One important tip to remember when installing your ecobee3 is to ensure that it is level on the wall. If it’s not level, the device’s sensors may not read the correct temperature, which could throw off the temperature of your home.

Connecting to Wi-Fi

Once you have installed the ecobee3, you can connect it to your home’s Wi-Fi network. This will allow you to control the temperature in your home from anywhere, using your smartphone or tablet. Simply download the ecobee app, create an account, and follow the instructions to connect the device to your Wi-Fi network.

Using Room Sensors

If you want to take your ecobee3 set up to the next level, you can use room sensors to control the temperature in different rooms of your home. These sensors can detect the temperature in a specific room, allowing you to set the temperature according to your preferences. This can be especially helpful if you have a large home with multiple rooms.
